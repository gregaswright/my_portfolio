North Atlantic Fishing Journal

An app for fishermen

North Atlantic Fishing Journal is an app designed for people who love fishing. A big part of becoming a better fisherperson is knowing when, where and how to catch fish, N.A.F.J is designed to help a fisherperson keep track of those very things. Utilizing Google Maps API provides a way for one to mark the location, time, and conditions on different species of fish that they have caught. This allows for a more organized and specific way to target different species throughout the year, and ultimately for better results on every fishing trip.
