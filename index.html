A little bit about me.

Software Engineer based out of the Bay Area

After ten years in the restaurant industry I thought that it would be close to impossible to change careers, but after taking a leap of faith I found something unexpected. Growing up in the Bay Area I have been always surrounded by technology, when I first began my career as a chef initially brushed this aspect of my life to the side, thinking it held little value in food. As time went on I began to see how technology, specifically software held great value in the day to day workings of a professional kitchen. As new software was introduced my opinion on what I liked and disliked about each one sparked a new passion in me. I found myself talking to coworkers at length about what features of a program I would change or add if I was designing the software. It wasnâ€™t until a coworker of mine told me that they were leaving the restaurant to go and start a coding bootcamp that everything seemed to click. I talked with my coworker about the program she was joining and her reasons for making the career change. That night I started shopping around on the internet for coding bootcamps and came across Flatiron School which had a good reputation for having a great curriculum alongside having excellent post graduation job placement. I lost my job when the pandemic hit which gave me a perfect opportunity to enroll at Flatiron as a full time online student. Flatiron gave me the skills that needed to turn my dreams of a career change into a reality.
